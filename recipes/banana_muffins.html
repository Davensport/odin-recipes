Banana Muffins

Description

These banana muffins are moist and delicious. This is an easy recipe for kids to help make and perfect for using up ripe bananas. They're wonderful as-is or with walnuts or chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large riped bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line cups with paper liners.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Mix bananas, sugar, egg, and melted butter in a separate large bowl until well combined; fold in flour mixture until smooth.
  4. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 2/3 full.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, about 25 to 30 minutes.
  6. Cool briefly in the tin, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
